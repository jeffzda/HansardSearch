About Hansard Search

Hansard Search is a full-text search engine for the Australian Federal Parliament's Hansard — the official record of proceedings in the Senate and House of Representatives, covering 1998 to present.

The corpus is built from XML transcripts published by the Australian Parliament House website. It includes 555,265 Senate speech turns and 630,173 House speech turns, enriched with speaker metadata (party, state/electorate, gender, Wikidata IDs) and indexed for fast boolean and phrase search.

Research use. This tool is provided for academic and journalistic research. The corpus is updated as new Hansard XML is published. For methodology details, see the Help & Methodology page.
